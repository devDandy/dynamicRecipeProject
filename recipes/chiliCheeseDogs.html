Chili-Cheese Dogs

It's to die for!

Half-Size

Regular-Size

Double-Size

Serves

4 (2 dogs each)

Prep Time

15 minutes

Coooking Time

15 minutes

Total Time

30 minutes

Level: easy

Ingredients - +

  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil, 1 turn of the pan
  • 1 pound ground sirloin
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire Sauce -- eyeball it
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder, a palmful
  • 1 (8-ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 8 fat or foot-long beef franks
  • 1 tablespoon hot sauce
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 8 hot dog buns, toasted
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar, 1
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions - +

  1. Heat a skillet over medium high heat.
  2. Add extra-virgin olive oil and meat and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Brown and crumble beef.
  4. Add Worcestershire, onion, garlic, chili powder and cook together 5 minutes.
  5. Add tomato sauce and reduce heat to low.
  6. Meanwhile, boil franks in shallow pan to warm through, 5 minutes.
  7. Drain water and return pan to medium heat.
  8. Score casings on dogs.
  9. Melt butter in skillet and hot sauce.
  10. Add dogs to pan, browning and crisping the casings in hot sauce and butter.
  11. Place dogs in buns and top with chili and lots of cheese.
  12. Place dogs under broiler and melt cheese.
  13. Serve immediately.
