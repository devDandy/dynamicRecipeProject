Chili Cheese Fries

This is an easy way to make one of my favorite snacks, chili cheese fries.

Serves

6

Prep Time

5 minutes

Coooking Time

20 minutes

Total Time

25 minutes

Level: easy

Ingredients - +

  • 1 (32-ounce) package frozen seasoned french fries
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons of water
  • 2 cups of low-fat milk
  • 1 tablespoon of margarine
  • 8 slices of American cheese, cut into pieces
  • 1 (15-ounce) can of chili without beans

Instructions - +

  1. Prepare french fries as directed on the package.
  2. Stir cornstarch and water in a small cup until cornstarch dissolves; set aside.
  3. Bring milk and margarine to a boil in a saucepan, stirring constantly.
  4. Reduce the heat and whisk the cornstarch mixture into the milk mixture, bring to a simmer over medium heat.
  5. Cook and stir until the mixture is thick and smooth.
  6. Add the cheese to the milk mixture and stir until the cheese has melted and is well combined.
  7. Prepare chili as directed on the can.
  8. Pour the cooked chili and the cheese sauce over the top of the cooked french fries.
